"DNS suffixes" are used for resolving unqualified domain names (names with no dots).

In the Windows Control Panel, double-click the "Network" icon:

In the "Network" dialog, select TCP/IP for one of your network connections and click the "Properties" button:

In the "TCP/IP Properties" dialog, select the "DNS Configuration" tab, and add your DNS suffixes under "Domain Suffix Search Order":
